Kyiv Airport Transfers

  • Is it easy to find a taxi at Kyiv airport?

    Taxi in Kiev airports: Boryspil and Zhuliany. Airport Shuttle services from the airports to the city center and other cities of Ukraine. There is also a taxi service back to the airport. Fixed price. We accept credit cards.

  • Where will my driver find me?

    The day before you arrive, you will receive an email from us containing your driver’s name, phone number, and a recent photo. Once you land at Kyiv Airport and go through the arrivals gate, your driver will be waiting for you holding up a sign with your name on it so you can’t miss them. You can also contact your driver via the Welcome Pickups app chat feature.

  • What happens if my flight is delayed?

    You really don’t need to worry about your flight being delayed or arriving earlier than expected. Flight plans are beyond your control and can change at a moment’s notice. We know this, and that’s why all of our Kyiv airport taxi drivers receive live updates on your plane’s arrival time. That way, we can guarantee that your driver will be there waiting for you when you arrive, at no extra cost.
